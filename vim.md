# Vim


## Movement

**Left,up,down,right**
hjkl

**start of line**
0 (zero)

**end of line**
$

**beginning of next word**
w

**beginning of next word, defined by white space**
W

**end of the next word**
e

**end of the next word, defined by white space**
E

**back to the beginning of previous word**
b

**back to the beginning of previous word**
B
